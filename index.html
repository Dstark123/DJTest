A Note from the Editor

Hello, everyone! Welcome to what I hope will become a regular, weekly newsletter. The inspiration for this started during last week's QBR. I was listening to the discussion about sharing information, and it got me thinking about how we could do it better. I even complained to Alexis about the lack of a simple newsletter to get information out! So, instead of waiting for someone else to create one, I decided to take the initiative myself and build something specifically for our team.

This is a new endeavor, and I'm still figuring out the rhythm, so please bear with me. The content, format, and topics are all flexible. If you have suggestions or anything you'd like to see included, please don't hesitate to reach out. My goal is for this to become a truly useful tool for our team, and your input is key to making that happen.

This Week's Wins

Scoops and Treats

Live in Just 5 Days!

This account landed on a Monday with the hope of an extreme turnaround time of that same Friday. While this is an unrealistic expectation, with some very quick communication between menu, trainers and hardware, we were able to devote time to quickly work on this account. Thanks to this coordination from all of our teams we were able to meet this merchant's expectations and have them live in just 5 days! While this not within our normal expectations it is amazing to see the teams all pull together and do some incredible work.
